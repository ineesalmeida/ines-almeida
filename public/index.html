Cell by Cell

When working in the Gulbenkian Science Institute (IGC), I had the challenge of building a videogame to young people what we did in our lab.

I created Cell-by-Cell in Unity using C# where the players would learn about Flow Cytometry, cell Sorting and sorting efficiency vs. purity. We also build a controller with a blue and a red button to make it more fun for kids.

Unfortunatelly, I didn't have the time to improve the art and add cool features before the release, but it was quita a fun and successful project!
